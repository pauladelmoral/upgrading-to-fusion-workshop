# migrating-dbt-core-to-fusion

Fix all error messages related to dbt-core when migrating to Fusion.

## When to use

When you are migrating from dbt-core to Fusion and encounter error messages related to dbt-core.

## Instructions

1. Review the error messages you are encountering during the migration process.
2. Identify the specific issues related to dbt-core that are causing the errors.
3. Use the following steps to resolve the issues:
    - Check for any deprecated features or configurations in your dbt-core project that may not be compatible with Fusion.
    - Update your dbt-core project to align with the requirements of Fusion, such as updating dependencies, configurations, and code.
    - Test your updated project to ensure that the error messages are resolved and that it is functioning correctly in the Fusion environment.
4. If you continue to encounter issues, consult the Fusion documentation or seek support from the Fusion community for further assistance.
